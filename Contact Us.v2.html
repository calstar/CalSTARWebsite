FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q. Is there an application?

A. No! We do not have an application, and we accept any and all interested members. STAR has championed our no-application policy since our founding in 2016. There is also no rocketry experience required to join the club.

Q. What is the time commitment each week?

A. We strongly believe that with STAR, the more you put into the club, the more you receive back from it. The recommended minimum time commitment is attendance of the weekly general meeting and a subteam meeting each week which are one hour each. Realistically, new members who spend 3-6 hours per week on the club are set to do well. The club is flexible with missing a limited amount of meetings per semester, as we understand that people may not be able to commit equal amounts of time every week due to finals and exams.

Q. What majors can join the club?

A. All majors can join the club! There are no requirements by major, GPA, year, or anything else to join.

Q. Does it cost money to join the club?

A. No! There are no fees to join or maintain membership.

Q. More questions?

A. We have a more detailed FAQ on our Gitbook site here . If you still have questions, feel free to send us an email at ucberkeleystar@gmail.com, or if you have more technical questions feel free to send an email to one of the subteam leads listed on the Team page!

ABOUT US
