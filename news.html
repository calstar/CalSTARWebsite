STAR Partners with CCTV Camera World for Hotfire Testing

JUN-13-2019

This Summer CalSTAR intends to perform a pivotal liquid engine hotfire. This hot fire is an important test of our liquid propulsion system and will help us evaluate its performance. The hotfire will provide a training opportunity for STAR’s Propulsion sub-team, who will use the experience to prepare for future launch opportunities.

LE-175 after coil wrapping

In order to enhance the Propulsion team’s ability to analyze its engines, we are proud to announce that we have partnered with CCTV Camera World to gain access to their expertise on choosing the right camera for our use. While there are a plethora of high quality cameras to choose from that can be adopted for our use, we narrowed down our pick to this camera. It is a 1080p Sports camera like a GoPro that will be used to record the hotfire in full and up close and give STAR and its members insight into the performance of our liquid engine prototype. The camera will also be used by other subteams in the future to create both promotional and data analysis videos, and potential on a future rocket flight.

Make sure to keep up with the Propulsion and its sister subteams on our projects and news pages!
