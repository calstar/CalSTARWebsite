Innovation



CalSTAR is an organization that pushes the boundaries of space technology. Our commitment to innovative ideas and efficient execution drives us to build unique, state-of-the-art rockets. Check out some of our past projects below.

ARKTOS

Height: 123"

Weight: 28.2 lbs

Year: 2018



Details


  • Location: Tuscon, AZ
  • Body Material: Blue Tube
  • Diameter: 4"/6"
  • Stability: 2.27 Cal
  • Payload: Solar Powered Rover
  • MAX Velocity: 620 ft/s (Mach 0.56)

Objectives


  • Target Altitude: 5280 ft
  • Successful Rover Payload Ejection
  • Autonomous Rover Movement
  • Solar Panel Deployment
  • Safe Vehicle Recovery
  • Live Telemetry

Outcomes


  • Altitude: 5364 ft
  • Fully Functioning Rover
  • Operational Payload Ejection
  • Nominal Flight Trajectory
  • Successful Avionics Data-Logging
  • Reworked Subteam Integration

ABOUT US

CALSTAR is UC Berkeley’s sole premier competitive rocketry team, founded in 2015 and comprised of a medley of audacious and wildly curious students with a burning passion for space.
